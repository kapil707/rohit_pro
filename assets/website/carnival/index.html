About: Spectrum Metro Sector 75 Noida

Under the aegis of SPECTRUM GROUP, Spectrum@Metro is a benchmark in commercial and retail development, a one of its kind project located at Sector 75, Noida.

SPECTRUM is a GROUP that is synonymous with quality, commitment, reliability and excellence in achievement. The group is recognized for delivering state-of-the-art ventures impeccable in design and outstanding in standards. We are a prominent real estate company engaged in handling a wide range of real estate projects including residential projects, serviced apartments and retail spaces with a prime focus on commercial spaces.

The recent launch of our commercial high street extravaganza Spectrum@Metro, is the perfect business proposition served on a silver platter. The commercial project with 1km long frontage spans across 6 acres and has scope for further development across 9 acres covering an area of approximately (371612.16) square meter. Set amidst the township of Golf City, it attracts a catchment of approximate 8 lakh population and is perched in the vital location of Noida. Bang opposite Sector 50 metro station and connectivity to Expressway, NH-24, and Delhi via road makes it easily accessible. The project which is ideal for business start-ups has already started transforming into a favourite destination across the commercial bracket.
